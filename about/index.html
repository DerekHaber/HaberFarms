About Us

Welcome to Haber Farms, your local farm in Eastern Jackson County dedicated to providing fresh, sustainable, and locally grown produce. Founded in 2025, we specialize in a variety of offerings, including nutrient-rich microgreens, artisanal sourdough bread, farm-fresh eggs, seasonal vegetables, and sweet berries. Our farming practices focus on reducing waste, ensuring that any products not sold are either preserved, enjoyed, or composted to benefit the next harvest. We are passionate about cultivating healthy food that brings value to our community and the environment.

Our Story

Who We Are

Our owners, Derek and Mayci Haber, decided in 2024 to begin planning for a small farm as soon as they would get property. In October 2024, they officially bought their house, and had enough yard for a small suburban farm. This led to the beginnings of Haber Farms in Janurary, 2025!
