About Us

Haber Farms, located in Eastern Jackson County, is dedicated to providing fresh, sustainable, and locally grown produce. Established in 2025, we specialize in a wide range of products, including microgreens, sourdough bread, farm-fresh eggs, seasonal vegetables, and more! We are a no waste production, meaning any product that doesn't get sold will be preserved, eaten, or composted to benefit the next harvest. Our mission is to cultivate healthy, delicious food for our community.
